# Research And Thesis Notes

This directory is the source of truth for research reasoning that may later be
synthesized into the LaTeX thesis.

It contains:

- source and literature assessments;
- research hypotheses and alternatives;
- functional-equivalence and validity reasoning;
- theoretical comparison matrices and example calculations;
- evaluation designs, assumptions, and evidence interpretation; and
- decisions that affect the scientific contribution or thesis scope.

It is deliberately separate from `docs-site/`.

The published documentation site serves users, operators, and developers. It explains
the current implemented system, setup, configuration, operation, extension points, and
known technical boundaries. It must not present open research hypotheses, proposed
thesis contributions, or theoretical evaluations as current product behavior.

The final prose belongs in `twin2multicloud-latex/`. Research notes remain supporting
material and must be reviewed, cited, and rewritten before they become thesis claims.
