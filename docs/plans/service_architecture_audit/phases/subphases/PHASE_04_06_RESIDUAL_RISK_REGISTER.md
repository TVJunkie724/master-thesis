# Phase 4.6: Residual Risk Register

## Purpose

Close Phase 4 with a clear list of what is thesis-ready, what is intentionally
deferred, and what still needs live-provider proof.

## Deliverables

- Residual risk table with owner, impact, mitigation, and GitHub issue number.
- Live E2E quarantine list and trigger conditions.
- Provider least-privilege unknowns and validation status.
- Final Phase 4 review summary.

## Acceptance Criteria

- No known high-risk service-layer issue is left implicit.
- Deferred risks are explainable in the thesis and actionable for a future
  developer.
- The roadmap status matches the evidence collected in Phases 4.1 through 4.5.
